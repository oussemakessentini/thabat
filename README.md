## 📖 About

**Thabat** is a cross-platform mobile application designed to help Muslims strengthen their relationship with Allah by providing practical tools to stay consistent in worship.

The application focuses on helping users:

- 🕌 Track and recover missed prayers (Qadha)
- 📖 Memorize and review the Quran
- 📊 Monitor long-term spiritual progress
- 🎯 Build healthy daily habits
- 🌍 Connect with a global Muslim community
- ❤️ Stay motivated through meaningful goals and achievements

Our mission is to build a modern, beautiful, and easy-to-use companion that encourages consistency while respecting privacy and sincerity in worship.
